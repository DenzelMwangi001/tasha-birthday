My Wishes for Your New Year

May this year unfold like a long, graceful melody — patient, rich, and kind. My wishes for you:

  • Grace to carry every burden lightly.
  • Joy that lingers in small mornings.
  • Clarity in your purpose and warmth in your home.
  • Surprising moments of laughter and rest.
  • Deepening faith and gentle restoration.

Signed with all I am — Denzel.
